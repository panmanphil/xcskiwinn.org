Membership

 You can join WINN by simply sending your name, address, phone number, and check* (see below), payable to Wisconsin Nordic Network, to:

    WINN
    2907 Farmview CT
    Waukesha WI. 53188

Anyone can join WINN. There are 5 different membership levels:  

  • $15- Individual Membership  
  • $25- Club Membership  
  • $200- Retailers  
  • $500- Manufacturers  
  • $100- Nordic Centers  

 *Note that until further notice, all dues and membership fees are waived. We currently have enough funds to last for a couple of years at our current budget.
You can also request to join WINN as an at large board member, volunteering your time to help grow our sport. Contact Us if you are interested.
