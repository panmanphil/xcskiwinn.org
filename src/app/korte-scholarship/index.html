Kortelopet Scholarship Program

 


 
 

 

Thanks to our donors!

Your tax deductible donation sends these 2 young skiers to the Korte on scholarship

This Program allows each WI High School Coach to name High School and Middle School Skiers who have not skied the Kortelopet before & would not be able to participate without your financial support.  Give a little to give a lot: the experience of skiing the Greatest Race On Snow!

This Program is organized by:

The American Birkebeiner® Ski Foundation

The Wisconsin Nordic Ski League, Inc (Coaches)

The Wisconsin Nordic Network (WINN)

 

Youth are the Future Participants in the American Birkebeiner®

Mail checks to: WINN Treasurer, W250N8945 Hillside Road Lisbon WI 53089
